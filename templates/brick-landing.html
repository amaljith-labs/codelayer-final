Weighbridge Integration

Seamlessly integrates with physical weighbridge hardware to auto-capture gross, tare and net weights — eliminating manual errors and disputes entirely.

  • Auto-fetch weight from hardware on vehicle arrival
  • Tare weight management per registered vehicle
  • Net load calculation and instant gatepass generation
  • Vehicle-wise weight history and dispute resolution log
  • Multi-bridge support for large sites

Plant Operations Control

Monitor every crusher, screen and conveyor in real time. Track material grades, throughput rates and downtime — all in one unified view.

  • Real-time production and throughput tracking
  • Input-output ratio analysis per machine
  • Shift-wise production reports and handover logs
  • Breakdown recording and downtime tracking
  • Grade-wise material output classification

Fleet & Dispatch Management

Comprehensive fleet visibility — from trip planning to fuel reconciliation — so you always know where your vehicles are and what they're costing.

  • Trip creation, assignment and live status tracking
  • Fuel issue and consumption monitoring per vehicle
  • Driver records, licence and document management
  • Vehicle-wise expense and profitability reports
  • Scheduled and breakdown maintenance alerts

Finance & Accounts

Fully unified accounting that syncs with operations automatically — no dual entry, no errors, no delays in financial reporting.

  • Auto-generated sales invoices from dispatch records
  • GST / tax computation and filing-ready reports
  • Vendor payment tracking and aging reports
  • Customer ledger and outstanding balance management
  • Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet and cash flow statements

Analytics & Dashboards

Executive dashboards give management a complete picture of the business — production, sales, fleet and finance — in a single glance.

  • Role-based dashboards for management and operators
  • Live production KPI widgets with trend graphs
  • Sales performance by customer, grade and period
  • Stock level alerts and reorder notifications
  • Exportable reports in Excel and PDF formats
